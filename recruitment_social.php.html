Recruitment and Social

Student aircraft builders knows the importance of social events to provide a relaxed environment where members can create lasting relations with one another and where members can enjoy a little time off from their busy schedules. As a result, SAB aspires to hold numerous social events throughout the year.

Meet, Greet, and Eat

To kick of the 2013-14 school year we are going to conduct a meet, greet, and eat at the SAB hanger. This event is exactly what it sounds like; members will have the opportunity to come visit the hanger, meet current or prospective SAB enthusiasts and enjoy some pizza.

SAB Half Price Burgers at Joe's

SAB invites everyone to join our weekly tradition of going to Joe's (706 S 5th St) on Thursdays, at 6pm for half price burgers (veggie burgers are offered as well). Come out and meet your exec board and fellow members! I Take a break from your work to enjoy a half-priced meal along with some great people.

Yuri's Night

Every year aerospace students celebrate the anniversary of Yuri Gagarin's trip into outer space! The Soviet pilot was the first human to make the journey and his spacecraft completed its orbit of Earth on April 12th, 1961. Any student who has been to Yuri's night will tell you it is the best night of the year; you do not want to miss it. We usually reserve a section of a bar and are given great deals throughout the night.

Prospective Events

We have a large amount of ideas for social events, however a lot of event planning comes down to time and money thus many of them will be labeled under prospective events until they are finalized.

Cookouts

Cookouts are definitely one of the more likely prospective events that will be put into action. Most of you have been to a cookout for a RSO on campus and for those of you that haven't, basically what the event entails is we choose a day that has nice weather and we fire up the grill and make delicious food. In a few words: good weather, good food, and good people.

Barcrawls

There will be numerous barcrawls SAB will be participating in throughout the year, but the dates will not be known until much later. A barcrawl is a night where a large group of students move from bar to bar on the hour (on a planned route) and enjoy a night to mingle, let loose, and maybe even sing some karaoke.

Capture the Flag

Capture the flag is game we all remember playing as kids. With the beautiful campus we have, I think a large scale capture the flag on a weather permitting day would be awesome!

Movie Night

Movie nights are self-explanatory. We will select a movie (most likely aerospace related) and rent out a room such as 103 Talbot or a room in the Illini Union. Also order pizza or other refreshments, and enjoy a laid back night with fellow engineering students.

Get Involved!

The above prospective events are just a list of some of the ideas we plan to implement. We have other ideas we are working with that are not listed above. I would love to hear any ideas or suggestion you have so please do not hesitate to email me at: pspatel2@illinois.edu.

Anyone interested in getting involved in planning social events for SAB please apply here to be the deputy social chair.
