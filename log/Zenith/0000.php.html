November 2, 2013

Drilling the Horizontal Stabilizer Skeleton

Builders: Luke Toczek, Jessie Lauzon, Andreas Brunner
Foreman: Andy Myrna

This marked the first build session for the Zenith CH 750 STOL and specifically for the horizontal stabilizer. This consisted of basic skeleton assembly and match drilling as shown below in Figure 1.

After completion of match drilling the stiffeners, some preliminary deburring was done.

There was one issue in which the drill was not positioned correctly on the pilot hole and a small indentation was made (Figure 3). To remedy this error, the indentation area was sanded (Figure 4). suitable. Phil Hayden, the aircraft maintenance manager at the Institute of Aviation, confirmed that our fix was suitable and the part is still structurally sound. With a project of this scale, it is expected that mistakes will happen, and we are very grateful that we have so many experienced people who are able and willing to lend us their expertise in instances like these.
