January 23, 2015

Finishing the Left Outboard Slat

Builders: Brian Read, Amy Zou
Foreman: Jessie Lauzon, Luke Toczek, Andy Myrna

The mis-matched hole was not as bad as we originally thought, and with careful drilling, we were able to finish drilling the holes to size 30. We deburred and then riveted, which finished the left outboard slat.
We left the four last rivets near the inboard slat undone, in case there needs to be adjustment in the positioning of the inboard slat, which is fitted inside the ouboard slat.

The only work left to be done on the slats is cutting and attaching the fiberglass end pieces to the slats.
