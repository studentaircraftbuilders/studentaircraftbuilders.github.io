Become a Sponsor

As our project depends entirely on corporate, university, and private donations, fundraising is a major focus of SAB. Any donation received will go directly to funding our efforts this Fall (and beyond).

We offer excellent sponsor exposure at all of our events, as well as providing access to our talented and driven engineers. Our benefits are outlined in the Sponsorship Package graphic below.

We are operating under the University of Illinois' Department of Aerospace Engineering, and as such, we possess active 501(c)3 status. We also welcome in-kind donations in the form of tools and equipment, which are also tax deductible.

If you or your company are interested in becoming a sponsor, or interested in learning more about our projects, please contact us at sab@ae.illinois.edu.
